The Creation of Bitcoin

In 2008, an anonymous entity named Satoshi Nakamoto published the Bitcoin whitepaper, introducing a decentralized digital currency. The first Bitcoin transaction occurred in 2009, marking the dawn of cryptocurrency.

Bitcoin's Early Adoption

Bitcoin gained traction as tech enthusiasts and cryptographers recognized its potential. By 2010, the first Bitcoin purchase was made—a pizza order costing 10,000 BTC.

Mainstream Recognition

By the 2010s, Bitcoin became a household name. It was used for transactions worldwide and inspired the development of thousands of other cryptocurrencies.
